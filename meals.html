Meals

Help with meal preparation, feeding and nutrition so your loved one stays well nourished at home. We can support with planning meals, shopping, cooking, and sitting with you at mealtimes—including assistance with eating when needed.

We take account of dietary needs, preferences, and any swallowing or consistency requirements. Our aim is to make mealtimes safe, enjoyable, and part of your daily routine at home.
