Our services

Flexible, dignified support tailored to each person and family across Scotland.

Personal Care

We support service users with personal care in the comfort of home, with dignity and respect. This includes assistance with washing and showering, oral care, brushing hair, and continence care—all delivered by our trained, compassionate team.

Companionship

Friendly, consistent companionship and emotional support so your loved one never feels alone.

Medication

Support with medication reminders and management, so treatments are taken safely and on time.

Meals

Help with meal preparation, feeding and nutrition so your loved one stays well nourished at home.
