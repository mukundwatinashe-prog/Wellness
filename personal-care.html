Our Services

Personal Care

Compassionate, dignified personal care delivered in the comfort of your own home. We support your independence while helping with everyday routines.

What we help with

We support service users with personal care in the comfort of home, with dignity and respect. Our trained, compassionate team helps with the following:

Washing & showering

Support with bathing and showering in a safe, private way that respects your preferences and independence.

Oral care

Assistance with brushing teeth, denture care, and mouth care to keep your mouth healthy and comfortable.

Brushing hair

Help with hair brushing and styling so you feel well groomed, comfortable, and confident every day.

Continence care

Discreet, dignified support with continence care and toileting, including use of aids where needed.

Tailored to you

All personal care is delivered in line with your care plan and your own routine. We work with you and your family to make sure you feel safe, respected, and at ease at home. Every visit is carried out by trained, PVG-checked carers who take the time to understand what matters most to you.
